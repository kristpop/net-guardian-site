Net Guardian

Passive Internet Speed & Net Neutrality Transparency

Net Guardian is a Chrome extension that measures the Internet speed you actually experience while browsing, and compares it against other users on the same ISP, in the same country, and on the same CDN and resolution.

What Net Guardian Does

Unlike synthetic speed tests that download test files, Net Guardian passively observes the websites and media you are already using (for example, YouTube) to estimate your effective throughput in Mbit/s.

  • Passive measurement ‚Äì no extra test traffic, only real browsing activity.
  • Percentile assessment ‚Äì see how you compare to other users on your ISP and in your country.
  • CDN awareness ‚Äì understand performance when streaming via CDNs such as Google‚Äôs video CDN.
Net Guardian is built to support citizens, researchers, and regulators who care about preserving an open, equal, and neutral Internet.

How It Works

When you click the Net Guardian icon while browsing, the extension:

  • Observes network activity in the active tab over a short period.
  • Estimates your average throughput based on bytes transferred and time.
  • Detects your ISP and country using a public IP lookup.
  • Identifies CDNs involved in the page (e.g., Google video hosts).
  • Optionally sends an anonymized measurement to the backend service.

The backend aggregates measurements from many users and computes percentiles, so your popup can show whether your throughput is in the top or bottom portion of similar users.

Data & Privacy

Last updated: October 2025

Information We Collect

Net Guardian does not collect personal data or browsing history. When you click Finish in the popup, the extension sends anonymized measurement data to our server for aggregate statistics:

  • Average throughput (speed in Mbit/s)
  • ISP name and country (from public IP lookup)
  • Video resolution observed during measurement
  • CDN hostnames (for example, *.googlevideo.com)
  • Basic system info (browser name/version, CPU cores)

How We Use Information

  • Compute throughput percentiles by ISP, country, and CDN + resolution.
  • Support research and transparency reporting about Internet performance.

We do not sell data, use it for advertising, or profile individual users.

Storage & Retention

Measurements are stored in aggregated form in a database hosted by our backend provider. No account identifiers, page contents, or detailed browsing histories are stored.

User Control

You can stop measurements at any time by removing the extension from your browser. Once uninstalled, no further data is collected.
